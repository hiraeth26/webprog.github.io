About Me



My name is Lucille Rose Ricardo, a filipino artist and an itroverted person who loves to draw, read and listening to music. Currently studying at STI Complex. Sta.Rosa Laguna, as a Multimedia Artist. I love making arts about the life of my silly characters with bright and warm colors, the reason why love to draw. Not because I am good at it, but to show my art as a story of how and why they become what their role were.

I don't rush in life but I let my time go on as we all pass, from a Musical Reference I will not let it rush, let me buy it's time and not throwing away my shot.

While I am not drawing on digital, I tend to draw and paint at my sketchbook and listening some musics on my free time. I am little fan of Musicals like Hamilton, Epic:The Musical, Six the Musical and 2024 Warriors. I am working with my other upcoming project which is the "Devilish Mayhem", that was still in draft and writing.
